About

GUILD_GEAR_SCHEMATIC.dwg

This event is about everything hardware and design, from soldering to designing PCB's to CAD. Don't know much about hardware? No worries-- we're going to be running many workshops so even beginners are welcome.

Here, you will create a product and pitch to our judges, and we will be annoucing categories for all skill levels to win some cool prizes!!

We're also going to have fun challenges such as spicy noodle challenge and karaoke to give your brains a break!
